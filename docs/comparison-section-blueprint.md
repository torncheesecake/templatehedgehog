# Template Hedgehog – Comparison Section Blueprint

This document defines the exact layout, composition, and intent of the homepage comparison section.

Codex MUST follow this. Do not improvise.

---

## PURPOSE

This is not a utility section.

It is a core selling section.

Its job is to make the difference between “build it yourself” and “use Hedgehog” feel obvious in seconds.

---

## SECTION ROLE

This section should feel like:
- a premium comparison feature
- a persuasive commercial block
- a visually distinct moment on the page

It should NOT feel like:
- a heading with two generic cards
- a table dropped into a section
- a neutral information block

---

## CONTENT STRUCTURE

### Intro
- Eyebrow (optional)
- Strong heading
- One short support line

### Comparison body
Two panels only:

#### Left panel
**Build from scratch**
- muted
- heavier
- more effortful
- slightly flatter treatment

#### Right panel
**Using Hedgehog**
- visually stronger
- cleaner
- more resolved
- slightly elevated

---

## LAYOUT

### Desktop
- Intro spans full width above
- Two equal-height panels below in a 2-column layout
- Clear gap between intro and panels
- Clear gap between panels

### Tablet
- Intro full width above
- Panels can remain side by side if comfortable
- If not, stack with Hedgehog first

### Mobile
- Intro full width
- Stack panels vertically
- Hedgehog panel first
- Build from scratch second

---

## PANEL CONTENT

Each panel should use the same row structure:

- label
- effort marker
- short explanation

Use 4 rows:
- Time
- QA passes
- Consistency
- Handoff friction

---

## VISUAL HIERARCHY

### Build from scratch panel
- darker or more muted surface
- less contrast
- restrained border
- subdued labels
- no strong accent highlights

### Hedgehog panel
- stronger surface contrast
- cleaner spacing
- slightly more elevated feel
- subtle positive emphasis
- this panel should look like the answer

---

## SECTION COMPOSITION RULES

- The intro must NOT be squeezed into a narrow left column
- The panels must feel grouped inside one composed block
- Add enough breathing room above and below the section
- The section should have a clear visual boundary from sections before and after
- Use spacing, tone shift, or elevation to create section identity

---

## DESIGN LANGUAGE

- Premium, calm, technical
- No gimmicky badges
- No loud warning styling
- No bright accent on the “build from scratch” side
- Hedgehog should feel like the resolved path

---

## FAILURE CONDITIONS

The section is wrong if:
- it still feels like two cards under a heading
- both sides feel equally weighted
- the left side is more visually interesting than the right
- the intro is narrow and stranded
- spacing feels cramped
