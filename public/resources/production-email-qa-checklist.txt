Template Hedgehog Production Email QA Checklist

Use this before importing compiled HTML into an ESP or sending a production lifecycle or transactional email.

1. Source control
- Keep MJML as the editable source of truth.
- Store the compiled HTML as a delivery artefact, not the main editing surface.
- Record the component or layout slug used for the build.

2. Content and links
- Confirm every CTA points to the correct production URL.
- Check merge tags, unsubscribe links, preference links, and legal footer copy.
- Keep critical message copy out of images.

3. Rendering
- Test Gmail, Apple Mail, and Outlook desktop before final send.
- Check mobile width, button wrapping, dark mode, image blocking, and footer legibility.
- Confirm background images are not essential to message comprehension.

4. Delivery handoff
- Import the final compiled HTML into the ESP or CRM.
- Re-check tracking link rewrites after import.
- Save a screenshot or preview URL for approval history.

5. Reuse
- If the block will be reused, make the change in MJML and recompile.
- Avoid repeated manual edits to compiled HTML.
- Move recurring variants into your internal component source.
