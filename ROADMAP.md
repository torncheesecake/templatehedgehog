# TemplateHedgehog Roadmap

## Future Backlog

- Build a dedicated ready-layout catalogue flow:
  - Let buyers preview full ready-made email layouts.
  - Let buyers select exact layouts before checkout.
  - Dynamically map pricing by selection count:
    - 1 selected: show `Single layout licence`.
    - 2 to 4 selected: show progress to `Five layout bundle`, for example `Pick 1 more to unlock the Five layout bundle`.
    - 5 selected: switch to `Five layout bundle` pricing.
    - 6 to 9 selected: show progress to `Ten layout bundle` and suggested upsell.
    - 10 selected: switch to `Ten layout bundle` pricing.
  - Keep clear bundle savings messaging and visible per-layout equivalent price.
  - Ensure keyboard accessibility, focus states, and shareable URL state for selected layouts.
